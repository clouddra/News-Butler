Plymouth University Raiders: Remain fifth after Wolves winWorcester Wolves suffered their first defeat of the season as they were edged out 74-71 at home by Plymouth University Raiders.Raiders came from 12 points down in the fourth quarter to score the upset win, with Trevor Setty leading all scorers on 27 points.The win leaves Raiders fifth in the standings, but level on points with second-placed Leicester Riders.Sheffield Sharks are third, also on 10 points, after cruising to a 90-63 victory over London Lions, led by 21 points from Michael Tuck.
