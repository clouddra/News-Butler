Worcester Wolves celebrated the first match at their new home court with a 73-61 victory over defending British Basketball League champions Leicester Riders.The Riders won a treble of trophies last season but could not spoil the party at the University of Worcester Arena on Friday night.Former Riders star Zaire Taylor scored 22 points and provided a dominant fourth-quarter performance to ensure the Wolves started the season 2-0.Elsewhere in the BBL, Sheffield Sharks went top of the standings with their third consecutive victory, a 75-69 success over the winless Plymouth Raiders.Jeremie Simmons and Michael Tuck each tallied 18 points, while Nick Lewis added 16 points and 13 rebounds from the bench.Matt Pressey was the Raiders' best performer, notching 19 points but being unable to spark a comeback in the final quarter.
