Rashad Hassan: Scored 16 points in Leicester Riders victoryDefending BBL champions Leicester Riders moved up to second in the standings as they defeated Glasgow Rocks 81-72 in overtime.Anthony Rowe and Rashad Hassan had 16 points apiece for the Riders, who dominated the extra period 14-5 after four hard-fought quarters.The Rocks were led by  EJ Harrison and Gareth Murray, who also finished the game with 16 points each, while Daniel Northern added a double-double of 13 points and 10 rebounds.The win improves Leicester to 5-1, two points behind unbeaten leaders Worcester, while Glasgow drop to 1-3.
