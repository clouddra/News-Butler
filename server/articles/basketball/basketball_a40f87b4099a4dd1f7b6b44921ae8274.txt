BLACKSBURG, Va. (AP) -- Virginia Tech has suspended Forward C.J. Barksdale the first three games of the season for a violation of team rules.Coach James Johnson said Monday that Barksdale ''made a bad decision'' but still fits prominently into the Hokies' plans. Johnson did not release any details about Barksdale's actions.The 6-foot-8, 235-pound Barksdale averaged 5.6 points and 4.7 rebounds last season. He started 30 games and his production improved significantly after Johnson benched him for a game at Maryland because he wasn't happy with his effort.Barksdale will miss games against USC Upstate, West Virginia and Western Carolina.
