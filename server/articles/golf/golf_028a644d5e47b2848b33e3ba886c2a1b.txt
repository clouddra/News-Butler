    Teenage golfer Charley Hull receives a lifetime membership from her home club, Kettering Golf Club, a week after helping Europe to a 
    
first Solheim Cup win on United States soil.
    The 17-year-old, who beat former US Open champion Paula Creamer 5&4 on the final day in Chicago, turned professional in March and finished runner-up in her first five tournaments.
    Club professional Kevin Theobald and long-time member Jill Horrocks talk to BBC Look East about the youngster's progress.
