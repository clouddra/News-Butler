F1 Midweek report - Ep 15The F1 Midweek Report looks back at the main talking points from the Japanese Grand Prix at Suzuka.For this week's show, F1 Racing Magazine editor Anthony Rowlinson and former McLaren mechanic Marc Priestley join Anna Woolhouse in the studio to discuss the fallout from Suzuka.Under discussion:* Red Bull's controversial decision to change Mark Webber's race strategy midway through Sunday's grand prix.* How the sport will remember Webber...and the reputation of Sebastian Vettel as the German closes in on his fourth World Championship.* Romain Grosjean's performance in Suzuka and what a difference a year makes.* The management structure at Mercedes and whether we should expect Ross Brawn to still be in charge of the team next season.Enjoy!
