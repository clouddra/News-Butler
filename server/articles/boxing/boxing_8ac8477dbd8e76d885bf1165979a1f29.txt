Jamie McDonnell: Set to return to action in ManchesterJamie McDonnell will return to action in Manchester later this month on the Carl Froch-George Groves show, live on Sky Sports Box Office.The Doncaster bantamweight has not fought since beating Julio Ceja to win the vacant IBF title in May.However, he was recently stripped of the belt by the sanctioning body for failing to agree to a defence in the allotted time.McDonnell joins a stacked bill, which also features Scott Quigg's WBA super-bantamweight title defence.Liverpool pair Stephen Smith and Rocky Fielding are both in action, Martin Gethin defends his British lightweight title against Anthony Crolla and Olympic Gold medallist Luke Campbell fights for the fourth time as a pro.
