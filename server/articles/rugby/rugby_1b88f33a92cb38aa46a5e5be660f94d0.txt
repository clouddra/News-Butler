Kevin Larroyer: Will join Hull KR on loanHull KR have completed the signing of France international Kevin Larroyer on a season-long loan from the Catalan Dragons.Rovers have paid the Dragons a fee for the 24-year-old forward, who will link-up with the Robins at the end of the Rugby League World Cup."Kevin is a good, young player and we are very excited to have been able to get him on board," said Robins head coach Craig Sandercock."He has a tremendous future in the game and myself and the rest of the coaching staff are really looking forward to working with him."I'd also like to put on record my thanks to the Catalan Dragons for allowing us to take Kevin on loan for next season."Rovers fans will get an early look at their new recruit when he turns out for France against Papua New Guinea this Sunday.
