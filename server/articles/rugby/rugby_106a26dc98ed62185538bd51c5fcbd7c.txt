Ofa Fainga'anuku will miss Amlin Challenge Cup matchWorcester prop Ofa Fainga'anuku must serve a one-week ban after his sending-off against Leicester last Monday.The Rugby Football Union took action against the Tonga international after he was dismissed for receiving a second yellow card in the Aviva A League game.Fainga'anuku will now miss Saturday's Amlin Challenge Cup opener against French club Oyonnax.But the former Glasgow Warriors player can return to club action from next Monday.
