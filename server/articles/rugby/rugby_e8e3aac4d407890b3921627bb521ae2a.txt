Sidney Rice: Seattle Seahawks receiver's career has been hampered by injuriesSeattle Seahawks wide receiver Sidney Rice will miss the rest of the season with a torn ACL.Rice had 15 catches for 231 yards and three touchdowns in eight games for the NFC West leaders this season.He left the field with knee and head injuries midway through the second quarter of the Seahawks' win over St Louis Rams in week eight.And the club have since confirmed the severity of Rice's knee problem, the latest in a long line of injuries to have hampered the South Carolina product's career.He is contracted for one more year in Seattle where he is due to earn a base salary of $8.5 million.
