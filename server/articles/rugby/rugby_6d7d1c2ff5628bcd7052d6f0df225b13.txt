Daniel Hourcade: New Argentina coachArgentina have named Daniel Hourcade as their new coach for their tour of Europe after Santiago Phelan stepped down on Monday.Hourcade, who has just led his country's second-string Jaguars to a fourth successive Americas Rugby championship title in Canada, will take on the role as Argentina prepare for the 2015 rugby World Cup in England.Phelan made his resignation official at a news conference at Argentine Rugby Union (UAR) headquarters in Buenos Aires.The Pumas begin their European tour with a Test against England at Twickenham on 9 November Nov.They will then take on Wales and Italy on the following two Saturdays.
