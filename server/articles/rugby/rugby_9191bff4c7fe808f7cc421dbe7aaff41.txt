    BBC Look North visit the Fiji Rugby League World Cup squad, who are basing themselves at a hotel in Scotch Corner for a pre-tournament training camp.
    But the team are eating breakfast, lunch and dinner at a local pub in the tiny village of Hudswell, just outside Richmond, after discovering that the pub chef is a Fijian woman.
    Look North speak with pub landlady Jackie Stubbs and chef Elenoa Tuvota as well as Fiji team manager Mesake Navugona and head coach Rick Stone.
