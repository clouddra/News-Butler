Ma'a Nonu: Rejoining Auckland BluesNew Zealand centre Ma'a Nonu has made his peace with Auckland Blues coach John Kirwan and will return to the Super Rugby franchise.Nonu, who upset the Blues when he joined the Highlanders this season, signed a two-year contract that will make him eligible for All Blacks selection for the 2015 World Cup."Ma'a has been in brilliant form for the All Blacks and we're thrilled to welcome him back to the Blues," Kirwan said in a joint statement with the New Zealand Rugby Union."He's one of the best midfielders in the world so to have someone with his abilities is a huge asset to the squad."
