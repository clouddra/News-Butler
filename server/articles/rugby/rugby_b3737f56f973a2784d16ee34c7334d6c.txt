Elvis Vermeulen: Will be absent from rugby for the next four months due to herniated discElvis Vermeulen will be sidelined for four months after being diagnosed with a herniated disc.The No 8 French international will undergo an operation on Thursday after tests conducted on Monday revealed the seriousness of the damage.His club Clermont revealed the information on Tuesday morning.The 34-year-old, who has 10 international caps, missed the 2007 World Cup with a similar ailment.
