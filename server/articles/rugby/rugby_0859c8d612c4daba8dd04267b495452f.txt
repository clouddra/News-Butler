    Scotland and Italy play out a thrilling 
    
30-30 draw at Workington 

     as both countries remain unbeaten in Rugby League World Cup Group C.
    Matt Russell and Kane Linnett tries put Scotland 14-0 ahead but Italy scored twice before half-time and had the lead going into the final stages of the match.
    Scotland and Brett Hellewell saved the best try till last to level the scores but a missed conversion and a scoreless final 10 minutes sealed the draw.
