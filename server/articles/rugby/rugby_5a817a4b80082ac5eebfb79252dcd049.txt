    England players and coach give their reaction after they get their autumn international campaign off to a winning start with victory over 
    
Australia at Twickenham.
    The hosts came from behind thanks to second-half tries by skipper Chris Robshaw and fly-half Owen Farrell to beat Australia 20-13.
    England head coach Stuart Lancaster praises his young team for their performance but says they will learn from the experience, while Australia head coach Ewan McKenzie is pleased by the effort of his side.
