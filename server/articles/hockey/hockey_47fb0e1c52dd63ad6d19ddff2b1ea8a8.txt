BOSTON (AP) -- Boston Bruins' left wing Loui Eriksson will be sidelined indefinitely with a concussion sustained on a hit from Buffalo's enforcer John Scott.Boston general manager Peter Chiarelli updated the winger's status in a release issued by the team on Thursday.Eriksson was issued a match penalty in Boston's win at Buffalo for charging and launching himself at the forward.Scott was suspended indefinitely by the league earlier Thursday.
