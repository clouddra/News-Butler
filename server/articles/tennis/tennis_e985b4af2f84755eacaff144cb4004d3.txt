Stanislas Wawrinka defeated Tomas Berdych 6-3 6-7 (0-7) 6-3 to clinch victory in the opening singles match of the ATP World Tour FinalsThe Swiss player did not drop serve throughout the contest and lost just three points on his serve in the deciding set as he completed the win in two hours and 25 minutes at the O2.Berdych dominated the second-set tie-break but was elsewhere blighted by eight double faults and could not convert any of the five break-point opportunities he was given.Wawrinka, who was playing in his first match at a year-end tournament, takes an early lead in Group A while Berdych faces an uphill task with Spaniards Rafael Nadal and David Ferrer still to come.
