Janko Tipsarevic: Wasn't able to force a break-point opportunity against his teenage opponentTeenager Karen Khachanov caused a major upset at the ATP Kremlin Cup, knocking out third seed Janko Tipsarevic to reach the last eight in Moscow.The 17-year-old thrilled his home crowd with a sensational 6-4 6-4 victory that saw him slam down 14 aces.
