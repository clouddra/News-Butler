Caroline Wozniacki: most recent of her 20 WTA Tour title came a year agoTop seed Caroline Wozniacki beat Sabine Liscki at the Luxembourg Open to reach her first final since March.The Dane saw off third seed Lisicki 6-4 6-4 and will take on another German, Annika Beck, for the title on Sunday.World No.9 Wozniacki, whose most recent tournament victory was exactly a year ago at the Kremlin Cup, has dropped only one set all week.Unseeded Beck will be appearing in her first WTA Tour final after beating Stefanie Voegele of Switzerland in the last four.Beck was leading 7-5 1-0 when Voegele retired.
