Bert Trick was prevented from running at Wolverhampton on Thursday evening after his price plunged from 33-1 to 5-1 off-course which alerted the British Horseracing Authority.On-course stewards gave Bert Trick's trainer Frank Sheridan the opportunity to withdraw the gelding 40 minutes before the race and he did so.Sheridan, who trains at Wolverhampton, took the advice after being told the horse would be under investigation by the BHA.Bert Trick had previously had three runs for Liam McAteer in Ireland, beating just two rivals, and had been off the track since August 2011.
