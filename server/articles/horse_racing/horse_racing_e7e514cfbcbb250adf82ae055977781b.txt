NEW YORK (AP) -- Cease beat stablemate Congressional Page by 1 3/4 lengths Thursday in the $72,520 allowance feature at Belmont Park.David Jacobson trained the top two finishers who went as separate betting interests. David Cohen rode Cease, the 8-5 favorite, to his fifth win in 22 starts. The 6-year-old gelding ran seven furlongs in 1:24.08, paying $5.30, $2.70 and $2.10.Congressional Page returned $4 and $2.60, and Talk Therapy paid $2.50 to show.
