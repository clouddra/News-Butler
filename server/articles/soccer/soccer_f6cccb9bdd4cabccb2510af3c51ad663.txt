Valencia left-back Jeremy Mathieu has been called up by France for the World Cup play-offs against Ukraine next month, the Primera Division club have confirmed.France face Ukraine in the two-legged play-offs on November 15 and 19 and Valencia announced on their official Twitter feed that Mathieu will come into the squad for the crucial fixtures.The post said: "Mathieu called up by France to dispute the two World Cup qualifying matches vs. Ukraine."The 29-year-old played his last game for France in the 3-0 friendly defeat by Brazil in June.
