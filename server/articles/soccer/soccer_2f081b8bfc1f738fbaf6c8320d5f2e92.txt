Russian billionaire Alexander Tsjigirinski has completed the takeover of Dutch outfit Vitesse Arnhem.Tsjigirinski bought the club from Georgian investor Merab Jordania, who revealed that he was happy to sell."When I bought the Vitesse shares I already stated that Alexander Tsjigirinski was supporting me and my plans with the club," he said."Now Vitesse have made some necessary steps for a bright future I feel it was the right moment to move on the shares to him."I will be available for Vitesse and Alexander to advise them in the future."
