Accrington goalkeeper Andrew Dawber has joined non-league side Clitheroe on a one-month loan deal.Accrington goalkeeper Andrew Dawber has joined non-league side Clitheroe on a one-month loan deal.The 18-year-old made three first-team appearances for Stanley last season but he has yet to feature on the field during the current campaign and has made the bench just twice.He made his debut for the Evo-Stik outfit on Tuesday night during their 2-0 win over Northwich Victoria before Stanley confirmed the loan spell on Thursday.Dawber signed a new deal at the Crown Ground in February which runs until the end of the 2014-15 season.
