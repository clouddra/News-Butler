Scunthorpe forward Matthew Godden has extended his loan stay at Dartford for another two months.Godden joined the Darts on a month-long deal in October and after four goals in as many appearances he will now remain with the Conference club until the new year.The 22-year-old previously scored six goals in 11 outings during two loan spells at Ebbsfleet.His Scunthorpe contract is due to expire at the end of the season.
