    Arsenal legend Dennis Bergkamp tells Football Focus that his "personal ambition" is to one day return to the club in a coaching role. 
    The former Netherlands international, who played for Arsenal for more than 10 years, describes the moment he decided to sign for the Gunners and his ongoing links with manager Arsene Wenger.
    Bergkamp adds that he can "understand" why people are finding comparisons between him and Mesut Ozil, saying he plays with the same "freedom on the pitch".
