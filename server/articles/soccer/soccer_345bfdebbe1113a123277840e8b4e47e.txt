Carlisle have completed the signing of defender Max Ehmer on a one-month loan deal from Championship side Queens Park Rangers.  The 21-year-old, who is contracted to Rangers until 2016, has agreed a switch to Brunton Park until December 1.Ehmer has previously had spells on loan with Yeovil, Preston and Stevenage.He could make is debut for the Cumbrians at home to Wolves on Tuesday night.
