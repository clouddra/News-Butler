Melbourne Victory's rising star Marco Rojas insists he is focused on the club's final run-in despite interest from Liverpool and Juventus.The New Zealand international is arguably one of the best players in the A League this term and his heroics for Melbourne have won him many admirers abroad.With his contract set to expire at the end of the season, the focus in recent weeks has been on the future of the 21 year-old."Nothing's happening at the moment," Rojas said on ONE News."As far as my personal decisions go, we're still going over it Dad (and agent Rodi) and me."Once the season's finished, I'll be able to speak about it a lot better."
