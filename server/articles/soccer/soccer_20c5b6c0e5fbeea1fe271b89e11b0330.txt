Eredivisie side Heerenveen claimed a 1-0 win over VVV-Venlo in the third round of the KNVB Cup on Thursday.Ramon Zomer scored the only goal of the game to put his side into the hat for the next round.The other match saw an upset as second-tier Excelsior beat Eredivisie outfit Go Ahead Eagles 1-0 thanks to substitute Moussa Kalisse's 90th-minute strike.
