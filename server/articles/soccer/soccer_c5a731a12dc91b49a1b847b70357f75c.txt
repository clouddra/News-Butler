    Newcastle goalkeeper Tim Krul tells Football Focus he cannot wait for 
    
Sunday's north-east derby at Sunderland.
    Krul says the fixture is the first Magpies players look for when the calendar is announced and that it does not compare to any other game.
    The 22 Premier League matches between these sides have produced nine Newcastle wins, nine draws and just four Sunderland victories.
     Watch the full interview on Football Focus, Saturday 26 October, 12:15 BST on BBC One, BBC One HD and the BBC Sport website.
