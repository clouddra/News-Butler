Martin Taylor has returned to Sheffield Wednesday at the end of his loan spell at Brentford.The 33-year-old centre-half joined the Bees in early September and made six appearances, scoring twice.Taylor's initial month-long loan was extended in October but he lost his place in the team over the last four matches.A Bees statement read: "All at Brentford FC would like to thank Martin for his contribution and wish him all the best for the future."
