Schalke midfielder Marco Hoger has been ruled out for the rest of the season with a partial cruciate ligament tear.The 24-year-old sustained the injury to his right knee during Saturday's 3-2 Bundesliga win at Eintracht Braunschweig and underwent surgery on Monday afternoon.Hoger, a mainstay in the Royal Blues' midfield since the end of August, joins Klaas Jan Huntelaar in the treatment room with a similar injury. The Holland striker went under the knife at a clinic in Augsburg on Monday morning.Huntelaar is expected to be back in action in January, although Hoger faces a six-month lay-off.Furthermore, Kevin-Prince Boateng and Jermaine Jones are also labouring with knee niggles which ruled them out of the weekend game.The Gelsenkirchen club host Chelsea in the Champions League on Tuesday evening.
