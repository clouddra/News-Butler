Scotland's friendly against Norway on November 19 will be played at the Aker Stadion in Molde.The Norwegian Football Federation confirmed the venue where Gordon Strachan's men would take on their hosts next month.The stadium holds 11,800 supporters and is home to the Tippeliga champions, who are managed by former Manchester United striker Ole Gunnar Solskjaer.The decision to switch the game from the Ullevaal Stadium in Oslo has been taken because of recent poor attendances there.The kick-off time for the game has still to be confirmed.
