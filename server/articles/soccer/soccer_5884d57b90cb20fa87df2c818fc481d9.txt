Mexican youth international Kevin Ramirez has been handed a trial by Manchester City.The 15-year-old is already currently in England, featuring in the prestigious Manchester United Premier Cup with his club side Chivas.But instead of travelling back to North America - he will remain in England and link up with City next week."After a great tournament in Mexico, Dennis (te Kloese, Chivas sporting president) talked with me and told me about the opportunity (to go to Manchester City), and I am grateful to the directors and the entire institution for this opportunity," he told Chivas' website."This is what you dream of as a child and you never imagine that you will get this chance at a young age.
