Barcelona's Jonathan Dos Santos will be out for around six months after damaging cruciate ligaments in his right knee.The Mexican midfielder picked up the injury in training, the Spanish champions said in a statement on their website on Thursday.The 23-year-old, the younger brother of Villarreal forward Giovani Dos Santos, will have arthroscopic surgery early next month.A product of the club's reknowned academy, Dos Santos is in his second year as a member of the Barca first-team squad and has made three league appearances this season.
