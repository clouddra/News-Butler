Lee Wallace: Back for RangersLee Wallace and Arnold Peralta are both set to return to the side as Rangers take on East Fife in League One on Saturday.Wallace has been struggling with a knee injury, and Peralta was unwell after reporting back from international duty with Honduras, but players are expected to be in the squad.Midfielder Dean Shiels is still missing with a knee problem while Ross Perry and Chris Hegarty are still ruled out out with ankle injuries.Former Rangers midfielder Steven Hughes may line-up against his old club and could be joined in the East Fife side by fit-again Christian Nade.
