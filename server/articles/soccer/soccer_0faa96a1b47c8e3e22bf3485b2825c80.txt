Sky Sports understands that Hull City are in talks to re-sign striker Gedo.Gedo spent the second half of last season on loan at Hull from Egyptian side Al Ahly and he played a major part in their promotion to the Premier League.The player made it clear he wanted to stay with the East Yorkshire outfit and now he could be heading back to the KC Stadium.Talks are ongoing over a new loan deal for the 28-year-old, as Hull could now seal a late move for the African ace.The new deal is again believed to be a loan offer, with a view to a permanent deal.
