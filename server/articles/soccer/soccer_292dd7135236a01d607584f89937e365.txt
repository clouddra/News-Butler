    Liverpool boss Brendan Rodgers admits Arsenal were the "better team" as the Gunners beat his side 2-0 at the Emirates.
    Santi Cazorla gave Arsenal the lead before Aaron Ramsey sealed all three points with a stunning second-half strike to move the league leaders five points clear of third-placed Liverpool.
    Liverpool have conceded in each of their past seven league games, having started the season with three successive clean sheets.
