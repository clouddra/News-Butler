Hoffenheim have announced the signing of Sweden international winger Jiloan Hamad.The Azerbaijan-born 22-year-old was linked with a host of clubs throughout Europe, but the Malmo star has agreed a deal to move to Germany.Hamad, who has scored 22 goals in 125 games for Malmo since 2009, will officially join Hoffenheim after the German winter break in January."Hamad is a young, ambitious and still developing player who has shown his qualities in Sweden," said Hoffenheim sports director Alexander Rosen.Hamad, who helped Malmo to their 17th title this season, has signed a three-year deal with Hoffenheim.
