    Paul Lambert is grateful for "another massive point away from home" after 
    
Aston Villa drew 0-0 at West Ham.
    The Villa boss says his side made enough "clear-cut chances" to win, after Christian Benteke came closest to scoring when he headed Matthew Lowton's cross against the crossbar from six yards out late on.
    Villa have now failed to score in their last four games, and the result leaves them only three points above the bottom three.
