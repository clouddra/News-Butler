Chelsea striker Demba Ba admits he is unhappy to have been dropped from the Senegal squad for their World Cup qualifiers this month.Alain Giresse's men face Angola on June 8 and Liberia one week later, but Ba will not be playing.Ba missed a penalty in his last appearance for Senegal, and Giresse has now opted to go without him."I am disappointed not to be called up, I want to play for my country and to be left out is painful," Ba told Sky Sports."I hope one day to tell the truth to the people of Senegal. I will always support anyone giving his sweat for the country."
