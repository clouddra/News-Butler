Kilmarnock's Rabiu Ibrahim has left hospital following his collapse during Saturday's game against Ross County.The player was stretchered off during the first half of the game at Rugby Park and underwent a series of tests during a two-night stay in hospital.A club statement said: "Rabiu has now been discharged and will undertake a period of rest prior to resuming training."The 22-year-old, who was signed from Celtic in January, lay on the pitch for several minutes before he was stretchered off.
