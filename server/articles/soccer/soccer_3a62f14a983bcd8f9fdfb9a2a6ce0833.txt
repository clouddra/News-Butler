Galatasaray have signed teenage winger Bruma from Sporting Lisbon for 10million euros, the Turkish club have confirmed.The 18-year-old Portugal Under-20 international, who was born in Guinea-Bissau, has signed a five-year contract with the Super Lig champions.Bruma made his Sporting debut in February and featured in 12 more Primeira Liga matches before scoring four goals in five games for Portugal at the Under-20 World Cup in Turkey this summer.
