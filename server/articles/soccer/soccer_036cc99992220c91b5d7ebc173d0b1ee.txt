Valenciennes have appointed Ariel Jacobs as their new head coach on a two-year contract.The 60-year-old replaces Daniel Sanchez, who was sacked last week with the club bottom of the Ligue 1 table thanks to a run of seven defeats in eight games.A statement published on va-fc.com read: "After ending their collaboration with Daniel Sanchez, VAFC have decided to entrust the job to Ariel Jacobs."The coach has won the Belgian championship twice with Anderlecht and won the Danish championship with FC Copenhagen last season."He joins VAFC for two seasons (plus the option of a further year) and will be presented to the press later (on Monday morning)."
